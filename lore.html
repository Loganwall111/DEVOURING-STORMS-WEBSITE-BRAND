Created by Logan Wall (Oneblockawayrewrittenteam). A multidimensional universe where Minecraft Story Mode's reality has been fractured and "rewritten." Multiple timelines exist — some broken beyond repair.

An entity / force that consumes dimensional energy. It targets specific timelines, absorbing them into a singular dark mass. Its true origin is unknown. MASSG was built to contain it — or was it?

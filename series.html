🌩️ THE SERIES

The Devouring Storms Universe — REWRITTEN MULTIVERSE

DEVOURING STORMS is a Minecraft Story Mode–related series created by Logan Wall (@REWRITTENTM) through the REWRITTEN MULTIVERSE. It follows a dark, interconnected narrative set across dimensions — where a force known as the storm consumes everything in its path.

The series features original music, cinematic teasers, short films, and an immersive Minecraft add-on (MASSG Add-On for Bedrock Edition) and Java Mod. Each video is a piece of a larger puzzle — part lore, part story, part universe-building.

Characters from the Rewritten Multiverse collide with the Devouring Storms universe, creating an ever-expanding world of secrets, battles, and broken realities.

THE GENESIS BREACH

The beginning of the storm. Something tears through the multiverse — a breach that shouldn't be possible. The world of Minecraft Story Mode collides with an unstoppable force.
